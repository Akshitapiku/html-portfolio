The Best Movies According To Akshita

My top 3 movies of all-time.

3 Idiots

The story follows the friendship of three students at an Indian engineering college and is a satire about the social pressures under the Indian education system.

KGF Chapter 2

It's a film that's high on style, heavy on action, and is carried by the powerhouse presence of its charismatic star.

Gangubai Kathiawadi

The movie which literally gives me goosebumps.
